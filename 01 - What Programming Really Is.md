- Has no common sense
- Cannot guess

A computer understands NOTHING unless you say:

1. Take a cup
2. Put cup on table
3. Add tea leaves
4. Heat water
5. Pour water into cup
6. Wait
7. Add sugar

## First Principle 3: Programs Are Just Instructions

At the lowest level, a program is:
- A list of instructions
- Executed one by one
- Exactly as written

No magic.
No creativity required yet.
Just clarity.

## Example 3: Counter Thinking (No Code)

Wrong thinking:
"Make a counter"

Correct thinking:
- Where is the number stored?
- What number do we start with?
- When should it change?
- By how much should it change?

## Practice Tasks (Do NOT Skip)

### Task 1
Write step-by-step instructions for:
**Turning on a light**

- Walk to the light board
- Identify light fan
- Change the state from off to on
- Light gets on

### Task 2
Explain how to:
**Open WhatsApp on your phone**
(as if explaining to a robot)

- Get the phone from pocket
- Open the phone by pressing the power button once.
- Enter the password for phone.
- Go to search bar and type Whatsapp
- Open Whatsapp by clicking the App icon.
- Whatsapp gets open.

### Task 3
Break down:
**Making Maggi noodles**
into tiny steps

- Go to the kitchen.
- Get the pan from drawer
- Get the water from vessel.
- Pour the water into the pan.
- Switch on the burner flame.
- Kepp the pan on burner.
- Get maggi noodles packet from the drawer
- Open maggi noodles packet.
- Break the noodles and masala packet.
- Wait for water to boil.
- After water gets boil, add masala to the pan.
- Wait for 1 minute, add the noodles to the pan.
- After each 30 seconds gap, stir it slowly.
- When the water is finished in the pan, switch off the burner.
- Maggis ready to eat.

### Task 4
Explain:
**Locking a door**
step by step

- Walkin to the door.
- Hold the gate door with your hand.
- Push the gate door.
- When the gate gets closed, pull off you hand.
- Now get the keys from the drawer.
- Insert the keys into the hole, which is in the middle of the gate.
- Rotate the keys two times.
- Pull the keys out of the door.
- Door gets locked.

### Task 5
Describe:
**Counting 5 coins on a table**
using instructions

- Start from zero.
- For each coin you see, increase the count by one.
- Final value is the total number of coins.

### Task 6
Write steps for:
**Refreshing a webpage**

- Place your first finger on the Trackpad
- Move the cursor to the top left of your screen.
- Click the refresh button.
- Webpage gets refreshed.

### Task 7
Explain:
**Adding 1 to a number**
without using math symbols

- Select the number.
- Increase the count of number by 1.
- Final value is the result.

### Task 8
Describe:
**Checking if a glass is empty**
in robot-level detail

- Selec the glass as seeing object.
- Check if there is water in the glass by seeing into it.
- If there is water in the glass, return true
- If there is absolutely no water in the glass, return false.
- If false gets returned, glass is empty.

### Task 9
Break down:
**Clicking a button**
into steps

- First identify the button that you want to click.
- Move your finger to the button
- Click it by pressing it once.
- Button gets clicked.

### Task 10
Write instructions for:
**Stopping a fan**

- Reach out to the lightboard.
- Select the fan button.
- Press the button downward, change the state from off to on.
- Fan stops rotating.
